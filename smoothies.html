Smoothies

Description

I love mangoes and fresh fruit. This is awesome to make and pack to have with you around town in a bottle, or just have for breakfast anytime.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup frozen mango pieces
  • 1/3 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup orange-mango juice blend

Instructions

  1. Combine the banana, mango, yogurt, and juice in a blender;blend until nearly smooth.
